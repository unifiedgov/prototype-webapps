You can apply for or renew a Blue Badge in England, Wales and Scotland using this service. For applicants in Northern Ireland, use the NI Direct website.

Before you start

You can apply for a Blue Badge yourself, on behalf of somebody else or an organisation. Depending on the type of Blue Badge, you'll be asked for the following information:

Self applicants or applying for somebody else

  • National Insurance number
  • proof of benefit or disability
  • proof of identity and address
  • a recent photograph

Organisations

  • name and address of organisation
  • charity number (if your organisation is a charity)
  • details of vehicles used to transport people

Your local council may call you in for an assessment if you don't provide enough information in the application form.

After you apply

Your local council will contact you to confirm if:

  • your application will be accepted
  • you're required to go for an assessment
  • your application will be refused

If you're refused, your local council should tell you why. You can appeal if you disagree with the decision. You can also re-apply if your medical condition or disability gets worse.

Questions

For questions about the Blue Badge scheme, contact your local council or the initial enquiry support service.
