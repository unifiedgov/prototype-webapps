You can apply for or renew a Blue Badge in England, Wales and Scotland using this service. For applicants in Northern Ireland, you can apply for a Blue Badge using the NI Direct website.

Before you start

If you're applying for yourself or someone else, you'll need to provide the following information:

  • National Insurance number for the applicant
  • Proof of benefit or disability
  • Proof of identity and address
  • A recent photograph

Your local council may call you in for an assessment if you're unable to provide enough information in the application form.

If you're applying on behalf of an organisation, you'll need to provide the following information:

  • Name and address of the organisation
  • Charity number (if your organisation is a registered charity)
  • Details of the vehicles you'll use to transport people

After you apply

Your local council will contact you to confirm:

  • if your application will be accepted
  • if you're required to go for an assessment
  • if they have refused your application

If your local council refuse your application, they should tell you why. You can appeal if you disagree with their decision. You can also re-apply if your medical condition or disability gets worse.

Questions

For questions about the Blue Badge scheme, contact your local council or the initial enquiry support service.
