If you took the photo on this device, you can continue and upload directly from this device.

On the page where you need to upload the document, there will be a link to "Select a file from your device". Press this, you can then select your photo, it may be in your gallery or photo library.

{% endset -%} {% set noHTML %}

If the photo isn't saved on this device you can transfer it, by either:

  • sending it to yourself by email
  • attaching a cable between the camera and this device
  • using Bluetooth to move the photo from your mobile phone or tablet to this device
  • scanning the document

Once you have the photo saved as a file on this device, you can continue.
